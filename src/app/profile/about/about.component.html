About

Before discovering my passion for programming, I enjoyed science and medicine. Graduating from University of Colorado Denver with the Bachelor’s degree in Biology, I worked as a laboratory research assistant and, later, as a medical interpreter thinking I would eventually continue medical education.
As great as it was, something was missing… That something was the fast pace, problem solving and unlimited learning opportunities that I discovered in programming. Having spent some time learning on my own, I enrolled into Flatiron School's Software Engineering Immersive program. The program was incredibly intensive and really pushed every cell in my brain! Now I have strong skills in React, JavaScript, and Ruby on Rails. Since graduation, I’ve been pushing myself every single day by learning and exploring new technologies. To build this portfolio, I used Angular and Bootstrap for styling.
